# Business Requirements

## Project Objective

Build a centralized retail analytics platform that enables business stakeholders to analyze customer behavior, sales performance, product trends, and operational metrics.

---

# Stakeholders

## Executive Team

Needs:

* Revenue reporting
* Growth analysis
* Profitability trends

---

## Sales Team

Needs:

* Product performance
* Monthly sales
* Customer purchasing behavior

---

## Operations Team

Needs:

* Order fulfillment analysis
* Payment trends
* Delivery performance

---

# Business Questions

The platform must answer:

1. What is total revenue by month?
2. Which products generate the most revenue?
3. Who are the top customers?
4. What is average order value?
5. What are monthly sales trends?
6. Which payment methods are most common?
7. How many repeat customers exist?
8. Which product categories perform best?

---

# Key Metrics

## Revenue

Total sales amount.

## Orders

Total completed orders.

## Average Order Value

Revenue divided by order count.

## Customer Count

Unique customers.

## Repeat Customer Rate

Customers with multiple purchases.

## Monthly Growth

Month-over-month revenue increase.

---

# Success Criteria

The platform should:

* Load retail data into Snowflake
* Support incremental processing
* Provide analytics-ready datasets
* Enable dashboard reporting
* Support scalable growth
